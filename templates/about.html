About Me

This is what I do.

Hey, My name's Roy, I'm currently a Systems Engineer in the Transport Industry trying to transition into the Tech Industry as a Software Engineer. I have a Bachelor's of Engineering, majoring in Electrical Engineering and another Bachelor's degree in Arts, majoring in Japanese and Chinese.

I didn't originally think I wanted to become a Software Engineer as during my university years, I very much disliked the aspect of coding in general and if you asked me back then whether I could see myself doing this, I definitely would have said otherwise. Fast-forward a couple years after graduation and my current company needed someone who had some coding experience. As I had studied Python and C during my university years, the coding tasks fell onto me and it was there that I found my passion for coding.

I'm not sure how to put it, but I know after spending 3 hours trying to debug my code, I instantly knew that this was the career path that I wanted to pursue. There's something about constantly wanting to scream at the computer screen asking "WHAT IS WRONG?" that seems somewhat satisfying to me and I really wasn't sure what it was that got me hooked.

It wouldn't be until a little later on into my job that I really wanted to pursue this path as I wanted to give Systems Engineering a good go before I hung up the towel. I'm more determined than ever to make this dream of mine a reality and I'm looking forward to what this future holds!
